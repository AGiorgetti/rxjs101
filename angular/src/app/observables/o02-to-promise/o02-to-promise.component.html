Convert the Observable to a Promise: we are down to ONE (1) web request!


The firstValueFrom() / .toPromise() operator subscribes the observable, cretaes and returns a Promise.

The Promise will be resolved when the observable complete.

The Promise will be rejected in case of errors.


It works but it has some drawbacks:

  • The web request will be made up front, even if there's no binding that displays the value.
  • We've lost the power of all the RxJS operators that can be used to transform the data.
